Biography
I'm a Ph.D student from the School of Computer Science in Shandong University. My supervisor is Prof. Changhe Tu. From Sep. 2017 to Sep. 2018, I spent one year visiting the GrUVi Lab in the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Hao (Richard) Zhang.
‍
My research focus on Computer Graphics, especially on the analysis and synthesis of 3D objects and scenes. I'm particularly interested in how to extract high-level understanding information and utilize it in 3D object and scene modeling.
